About Us

Home / About

Our Mission

The Electronic Music Council (EMC) is a community-driven initiative working to generate and maintain a positive, sustainable electronic music economy by facilitating opportunities for growth and development in Cork.

We believe that electronic music has the power to bring people together, create cultural exchange, and provide economic opportunities for artists, venues, and related businesses.

Through a combination of events, education, and advocacy, we aim to establish Cork as a vibrant hub for electronic music culture in Ireland and beyond.

Our Values

Community

We foster an inclusive community where everyone feels welcome, respected, and valued, regardless of background or experience level.

Creativity

We champion artistic expression and innovation in electronic music, pushing boundaries and exploring new sonic territories.

Sustainability

We are committed to building a sustainable ecosystem for electronic music in Cork that benefits artists, venues, and the community.

Education

We provide learning opportunities for aspiring artists, producers, and industry professionals to develop their skills and knowledge.

Our Story

2020

The Beginning

The Electronic Music Council was formed by a group of passionate music lovers, DJs, and producers in Cork who saw the need for a more organized approach to developing the local electronic music scene.

2021

First Initiatives

EMC launched its first series of events, workshops, and community gatherings, establishing a foundation for future growth and collaboration.

2022

Growing the Network

The council expanded its reach by forming partnerships with local venues, educational institutions, and cultural organizations to strengthen the electronic music ecosystem in Cork.

2023

New Era

2023 brought cautious optimism as EMC worked to overcome challenges and continue fostering the electronic music community despite changing landscapes.

2024-25

Expanding Horizons

EMC continues to grow its influence, hosting larger events, collaborating with international artists, and representing Cork's electronic music scene on the global stage.

Meet Our Team

Nat Mac

Head of EMC / DJ Promoter

NAT MAC has been bringing her unique style of mixing a wide variety of genres and with her crew outlawed bringing high energy dnb to the masses. As the Head of EMC, Nat has been instrumental in building bridges between artists, venues, and audiences throughout Cork's electronic music community.

Gwada Mike

Treasurer / DJ Promoter

Mike has been djing for years as Guada Mike bringing reggae, afrobeat and tropical house to the Cork music scene. As Treasurer of the Electronic Music Council, Mike helps ensure the organization can fund and support events that enrich Cork's electronic music culture.

James Walshe

Events Coordinator / DJ

James brings his extensive knowledge of electronic music and event management to the EMC team, specializing in techno and progressive house. His dedication to creating immersive event experiences has helped establish EMC as a cornerstone of Cork's electronic music scene.

Dan-Jo Relihan

Marketing Specialist / DJ

Dan-Jo brings a fresh perspective to the EMC team with his innovative approach to electronic music promotion and community building. His background in marketing combined with his passion for music helps EMC reach new audiences across Cork and beyond.

Miko

Miko's diverse musical influences and connections within Cork's electronic music scene make them a vital part of the EMC community. Their focus on inclusivity and creating welcoming spaces has helped EMC foster a diverse and vibrant community of music lovers.

VLR
