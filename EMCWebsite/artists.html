The Heart of EMC

The Electronic Music Council is powered by passionate DJs, producers, and promoters who are dedicated to cultivating Cork's vibrant electronic music scene. Meet the artists who make it all happen.

Nat Mac

DJ Promoter / Head of EMC

NAT MAC has been bringing her unique style of mixing a wide variety of genres and with her crew outlawed bringing high energy dnb to the masses.

As the Head of EMC, Nat has been instrumental in building bridges between artists, venues, and audiences throughout Cork's electronic music community.

Upcoming Events

Gwada Mike

DJ Promoter / Treasurer of EMC

Mike has been djing for years as Guada Mike bringing reggae, afrobeat and tropical house to the Cork music scene.

As Treasurer of the Electronic Music Council, Mike helps ensure the organization can fund and support events that enrich Cork's electronic music culture.

Upcoming Events

James Walshe

DJ / EMC Events Coordinator

James brings his extensive knowledge of electronic music and event management to the EMC team, specializing in techno and progressive house.

His dedication to creating immersive event experiences has helped establish EMC as a cornerstone of Cork's electronic music scene.

Upcoming Events

Dan-Jo Relihan

DJ / Marketing Specialist

Dan-Jo brings a fresh perspective to the EMC team with his innovative approach to electronic music promotion and community building.

His background in marketing combined with his passion for music helps EMC reach new audiences across Cork and beyond.

Upcoming Events

Miko

DJ / Community Relations

Miko's diverse musical influences and connections within Cork's electronic music scene make them a vital part of the EMC community.

Their focus on inclusivity and creating welcoming spaces has helped EMC foster a diverse and vibrant community of music lovers.

VLR

DJ / Technical Director

VLR brings technical expertise and cutting-edge sound design to the EMC team, specializing in experimental electronic music and immersive audio experiences.

Their attention to sonic detail ensures that EMC events deliver exceptional audio quality and innovative soundscapes.
