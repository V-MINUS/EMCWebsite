    Frequently Asked Questions

    How can I get involved with the Electronic Music Council?

    +

    There are many ways to get involved! You can volunteer at our events, join our mailing list, attend our workshops and meetups, or propose a collaboration. Fill out our contact form, and we'll get back to you with opportunities that match your interests.

    I'm an artist/DJ looking to perform at your events. How can I submit my music?

    +

    We're always interested in hearing from talented artists! Please use our contact form and select "Artist Submission" as the subject. Include links to your music (SoundCloud, Mixcloud, etc.) and any relevant information about your experience and style.

    Does the EMC offer educational programs or workshops?

    +

    Yes, we regularly organize workshops, masterclasses, and educational programs covering various aspects of electronic music production, DJing, event management, and industry skills. Subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on upcoming educational events.

    How can venues partner with the Electronic Music Council?

    +

    We're always looking to establish relationships with venues that support electronic music. Please contact us using the form and select "Venue Partnership" as the subject. Our team will reach out to discuss potential collaborations, events, and promotional opportunities.
